Calling All Trick or Treaters!

Greetings, all ghosts and ghouls! As Halloween rolls right around the corner, we hope to see you participating in its many wonderous festivities! Hover your mouse over the images below to learn more about fun times you could be having this October!


The kids in this image are trick-or-treating, an event where they go from door to door on Halloween (October 31st) asking for candy, exclaiming the phrase: "trick-or-treat!"

Enjoy family fun with a Halloween movie night! Choose a Halloween-appropriate film from scary horror movies, thrillers, or other fun family-friendly options. Enhance your experience with a variety of snacks, candy, and decorations to create a festive atmosphere.

Put your courage and bravery to the test by navigating through a haunted house! Depicted in the image, Fright Farm in Smithfield, PA offers numerous Halloween themed attractions like haunted mazs, hayrides, live entertainment, festival foods, games, and other fun activities.

Carve and paint a pumpkin! The pumpkins, as shown in the image, can be carved with your own custom designs and painted to add a pop of color. They also make great decorations on your front porch to display for people passing by.
